Candidates for Goshen Town office

Audrey Blondin has been a practicing attorney in Torrington since 1980 and currently practices along with her daughter Rose Blondin Shea. Originally from the New Haven area, she has been a resident of the Northwest Corner for over 40 years and previously served as a member of Litchfield Board of Selectman from 1993 to 2003. She has also served as the 30th District Committeewoman for the Connecticut Democratic Party since 2002 and also currently serves as the Secretary of the Connecticut Democratic Party. She also holds a Master of Public Health Degree and is an adjunct Professor of Law and Public Health at the University of New Haven. To contact Audrey email: audrey@blondinlaw.com.
