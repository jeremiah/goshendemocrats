Audrey Blondin

Board of Assessment Appeals


Audrey grew up in the New Haven area and has been a resident of the Northwest Corner for over 40 years. She served as a member of the Litchfield Board of Selectman for 10 years from 1992-2003. She has represented the 30th State Senate District on the Democratic State Central Committee since 2002 and also currently serves as the Secretary of the Connecticut Democratic Party. Audrey has been a practicing Attorney in Torrington for over 40 years. She and her daughter Rose have Litchfield County’s only mother-daughter law firm, Blondin Law Office, LLC, where they practice Real Estate, Elder Law, Wills & Probate, Bankruptcy and LLC & Business Law.

Audrey also holds a Masters Degree in Public Health from UCONN and is an Adjunct Professor teaching Law and Public Health at the University of New Haven. She and her husband, Torrington Optometrist Dr. Matthew Blondin are the founders of VOSH-CT (Volunteer Optometric Services to Humanity) and have served on missions for over 20 years in both Nicaragua and Virginia, helping to provide eye care and glasses to over 50,000 patients in need. They have 3 children, Dr. Nicholas Blondin, Dr. Joseph Blondin and Attorney Rose Blondin Shea and 6 grandchildren, along with a foster son Orlando Sevilla from Nicaragua and 3 additional grandchildren.

Dexter Kinsella

Selectman and Constable


I am a Goshen native who in 2002, after a seventeen year stay in Boston, returned with my wife to my childhood home to farm and make Goshen our home. I became involved in local politics out of a desire to serve and to carry on the tradition of community involvement and volunteerism which are hallmarks of this great town.

I have served as a Selectman for eight years, and before that on the Board of Finance as well as many civic groups and organizations throughout my life here in Goshen. It is my hope to continue to encourage greater community involvement through participation in the many groups and civic organizations as well as local government.

We are better when we work together.
